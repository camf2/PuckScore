Close-Game Clutch
1-goal W% is the KenPom stat
Regression across 10 seasons: 1-goal game win% correlates with playoff round advancement at r=0.61 — stronger than any offensive stat.
The KenPom parallel: KenPom weights close games differently from blowouts. A 20-2 record in 1-goal games signals fundamentally different playoff DNA than winning big and losing tight.

Special Teams
PP/PK net is compounding
Playoff refs call 12–17% more deliberate penalties. A +5 net ST edge generates ~1.8 extra PP goals per 7-game series — nearly a full game of structural advantage.
Why it snowballs: Teams that kill penalties effectively preserve leads and force risky offensive play — which generates more penalties. The cycle compounds over 4–7 games.
